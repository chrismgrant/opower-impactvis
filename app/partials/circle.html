I worked on defining and refining the user experience for the holistic Circle experience and the tailoring of the Android experience. I created user interfaces and features to achieve these goals.

Circle V3 UX & Android Front-end

I was responsible for helping to define the user experience of the Circle v3 app. This was Circle's first step towards utilizing the local interest graph to create better experiences that connect users to both their friends and what their city has to offer. My responsibilities varied from feature ideation, analysis, handling redundancy and other issues of the user flow, highlighting usability issues, and contributing to the Android front-end codebase. My process primarily consisted of sketching my concepts and then prototyping on the Android OS app. On the visual design side, I assisted in asset creation and filtering of city landscape photos.
