Highmark BCBS Bootstrap

Highmark’s goals with their redesign were to solve usability issues and promote a modern and consistent design language. The bootstrap that I helped to develop saved on engineering efforts and allowed brand consistency across Highmark’s various web presences.

Highmark Spending Accounts Redesign

Highmark wanted to keep some legacy assets in it’s redesign. Some of these assets had architectural limitations due to development environment. One such asset was the Highmark Spending Accounts website. I was tasked with the development and graceful degradation of this site in consistency with the new design language. Due to the client's third party web vendor templating, I was restricted to solely using CSS to accomplish this redesign. One of the biggest challenges was supporting IE 7.
