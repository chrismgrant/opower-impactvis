At Jawbone, my work primarily consisted of research on behavior tracking and behavior change applications and conceptual flows, along with keynote prototypes of interactions for proposed features in the Jawbone UP app, namely “Today I Will” — a motivational goal creation feature. My other work at Jawbone consisted of front-end development and content updates for the Jawbone support site's redesign and troubleshooting guides.

"Today I Will" feature for Jawbone UP

(active goal creation concept)

As a design intern, my main project was the Today I Will goal tracking and discovery feature for the new Jawbone UP app; this entailed design inquiry and user experience methods for understanding goal creation, discovery, and encouragement, along with behavior changing methodologies. I conducted research by analyzing behavior changing apps, such as Fig, and creating ux and content audits. From there, after looking at how such a system could work in the UP app’s environment and design language, I created concept flows and a keynote prototype of my proposed interactions. This work assisted in the final "Today I Will" feature.
