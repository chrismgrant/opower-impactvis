CMU Housing Finder

For the CMU SCS course, "Technical Communications for Computer Scientists", students form groups for the entire semester to create solutions to major problems of their choice, while also exercising technical communications skills, such as proposal drafting. My team proposed a solution to Carnegie Mellon's housing system and roommate matching processes through a housing and roommate finder dashboard app. As the team's ux designer, I followed an iterative design process, where I A/B tested and improved upon the proposed design, over the course of the semester, followed by a final prototype.
